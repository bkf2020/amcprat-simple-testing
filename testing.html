A page that gives links to all problems for testing.

How to use it?

  • Search for the test you want by pressing Control-F (or the hotkey to search for text). Example: 2021 AMC 10A

  • Right click on the link (which has a pink color) and press open in new tab.

  • Search for the test you want on Art of Problem Solving. For example, you may search for 2021 AMC 10A on Art of Problem Solving.

  • Then compare the two webpages (the first one on this website, the other one on Art of Problem Solving), and make sure the problems match.
